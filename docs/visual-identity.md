# Visual Identity & Brand Guidelines

DietCode's visual identity is designed to reflect its core philosophy: **Minimalist, Native, and High-Fidelity.** It prioritizes functional clarity over decorative flourish.

## 🎨 Color Palette

- **Diet Green (#4CAF50)**: The primary brand color. Represents growth, freshness, and efficiency.
- **Deep Slate (#121212)**: The primary background color for dark mode. Provides a neutral, low-strain canvas.
- **Pure White (#FFFFFF)**: Used for high-contrast accents and core text.
- **System Accents**: DietCode leverages native macOS system colors for menus, buttons, and focus states to ensure a seamless OS experience.

---

## 📐 The Logo

The DietCode logo is a geometric synthesis of the letter **'D'** and code-authoring symbols.

- **The Arc**: Represents the "D" for DietCode, drawn with a thick, rounded stroke for a friendly yet professional feel.
- **The Chevron (>)**: A classic symbol for command-line execution and progress.
- **The Cursor (_)**: Represents the active state of creation and the IDE's primary interface.

### Usage Guidelines
- **Spacing**: Maintain a clear margin of at least 25% of the logo's width around all sides.
- **Backgrounds**: The logo should be placed on high-contrast backgrounds (Deep Slate or Pure White). Avoid busy patterns.

---

## 🔡 Typography

- **Primary Interface**: San Francisco (Native macOS font).
- **Editor & Terminal**: Menlo or SF Mono (Fixed-width, high-readability).

DietCode avoids custom web-fonts to minimize binary size and startup latency, relying entirely on the host operating system's typography engine.

---

## 🖼️ Iconography

Icons in DietCode follow the **Apple SF Symbols** design language. They are monochrome, weight-matched to the surrounding text, and use semantic variants (e.g., `folder.fill` for the active sidebar).
